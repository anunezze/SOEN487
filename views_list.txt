/user GET
	- get all users
/user POST (username:string)
	- creates a username
*********************************************************
/conversation POST (creator_id: num, participant_id: num)
	- creates a conversation
/conversation GET
	- gets all conversations
************************************************
/message POST (conversation_id: num, sender_id: num, text: string)
	- creates a message
/message/<conversation_id> GET
	- gets all messages for a conversation
